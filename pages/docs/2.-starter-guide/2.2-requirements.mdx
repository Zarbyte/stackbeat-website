## Hardware

* **CPU:** Anything.
* **RAM:** 35 MB
* **Disk:** 1 MB. Add space for persistent storage. *"Real-time mode"* will not store stats.

## Network

Roughly 50 to 100 KB per tick depending on enabled metrics.

## Operating systems

All current Linux and Windows operating systems are supported.

This list is just the operating systems we have actually tested on. An OS not being here does not mean it will not work.

Report known working operating systems to us and we can add them to this list.

* AlmaLinux 9
* Ubuntu 22.04
* Fedora 40
* Linux Mint 22
