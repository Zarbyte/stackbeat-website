## Location

Visit `http://MAIN_ADDRESS_HERE:9999` to access the management interface.

## Authentication

The current internal release does not support authentication. The interface is open to the public.

<Callout type="info" emoji="ℹ️">
  Use a firewall to restrict access to `Port 9999` to provide some protection until future releases.
</Callout>
